\section{Example Governance Protocol for Layer~15 Morphogenetic Actions}
\label{app:governance_example}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item A proposed morphogenetic action $T$ is submitted to the \texttt{ReversibilityChecker}.
    \item If $\varepsilon$-reversibility is certified, the action is approved and logged; otherwise, it is escalated.
    \item For escalated actions, a multi‑stakeholder council reviews the counterfactual simulations and the normative concordance scores.
    \item A supermajority vote is required for irreversible actions; otherwise, the action is rejected or modified.
    \item All decisions, including dissenting opinions, are immutably logged and auditable.
\end{enumerate}
